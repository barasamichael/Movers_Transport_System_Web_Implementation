our services

We provides transportation for various agricultural goods for farmers between farmers and retail outlets. We transport goods such as:

  • cereals
  • livestock
  • perishable goods such as milk, eggs, fish and flowers
We also also transport farm inputs such as:
  • fertilizers
  • manure
  • herbicides
  • fungicides
  • seeds for planting

registration requirements

Large scale farmers register individually as our members while small scale farmers are required to form groups in order to qualify for membership. A group can have a minimum of five members and is recognized by a group name, location, nature of produce and details of individual farmers. The group can then order for transport as a unit.
